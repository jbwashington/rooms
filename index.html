About the House

Located behind Harp's off Wedington, with a Tan bus stop right around the corner. The house is 3 bedroom/2 bath--for rent, 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom. There rest of the house includes: living room (with plenty of room for your furniture), kitchen with dining nook, sun room, and large back yard with privacy fence--monthly lawn care is included in the bill total below. Please, no smoking inside, but feel free to use the back patio area.

There's a full-sized washer and dryer and also a dishwasher, so if you like clean clothes and clean dishes with minimal manual labor, you're in luck! If you need storage space, there's some of that, too. There is WiFi, which is included in the monthly bills, but no cable TV--I do all my TV watching online.

I have dishes, a blender, a grill, a fire pit, and a Crock Pot that I don't mind sharing. Forgot about the toaster oven, there is a toaster oven. Last but not least, there are two cats who run the show, which includes a lot of napping in the sun and waiting to be petted / loved upon.



About Me

I'm an early-30s non-traditional student who works. When I'm not at school or work, I'm home frequently, and I'm quiet and clean. On a cleanliness scale of 1 to 10, with 1 being a total slob and 10 being neurotically clean, I am around an 8--I pick up after myself, I don't leave dirty dishes in the sink overnight. I don't have any items around the house that don't have a specific place to be. I recycle...and ideally, you do too.

Outside of work and classes, I'm home often. I do web development and putter around in the yard in my free time, and when I have guests during the week (only one or two people), it is always before 10pm, as I maintain a regular work schedule. Other activities I enjoy include basketball, traveling, gardening/yard work, and volunteering/community service. Lastly, I'm in a long-distance relationship and my girlfriend visits every month or so, but our partying days are over so you won't even notice when she's there.
